About Wayz

Our Vision

To excel as the leading table water factory in Nigeria, delivering superior services and providing exceptional quality of bottled water.

Our Mission

Our mission at Wayz water is to provide refreshing and purified hydration that surpasses expectations. Through innovation, sustainability, and unwavering commitment to quality, we strive to deliver the purest and most satisfying drinking water experience to our valued customers.

Our Objective

Our objective at Wayz water is to be the trusted leader in premium-quality bottled water in Owerri and Nigeria as a whole, delivering refreshing and reliable hydration that exceeds customer expectations.
